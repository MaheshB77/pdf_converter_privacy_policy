Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to PDF Converter. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Local Data Processing

Our PDF Converter app processes images and converts them to PDF format entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your images or converted PDF files to our servers.

Usage Information

We may collect minimal usage analytics to improve our app, including:

  • App version information
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports (if any)
  • General usage patterns (number of conversions, feature usage)

How We Use Your Information

Since all your images and PDF files are processed locally on your device, we cannot access or use this content. Any usage information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Ensure compatibility with iOS updates

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your images and converted PDF files are stored locally on your device. You have complete control over this data and can delete it at any time through the app or your device's storage settings.

No Server Storage

We do not store any of your personal files, images, or PDFs on our servers. Your content never leaves your device during the conversion process.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services may collect limited technical information as described in their respective privacy policies. We ensure that no personal content (images or PDFs) is shared with these services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable analytics in your device settings
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions
  • Request information about what data we may have collected

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all processing is done locally and we don't collect personal content, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the App Store update notes or within the app itself.
