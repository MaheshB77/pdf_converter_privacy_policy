Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes! All image processing and PDF conversion happens locally on your device. We never upload or store your files on our servers.

Why doesn't the app work offline?

The core conversion functionality works completely offline. You only need an internet connection for initial app download and updates.

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPEG, PNG, HEIC, TIFF, and more.

How do I delete converted PDFs?

You can delete PDFs directly within the app or through your device's Files app. All files are stored locally on your device.
